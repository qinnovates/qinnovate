QIF-T0042

medium

BLE/RF side-channel

Extract neural data from BLE/RF emissions via timing, power, or EM side-channels. All wireless BCIs vulnerable. Consumer devices (Muse, EMOTIV) transmit unencrypted. Merges ONI-T002 (Side-Channel Analysis).
