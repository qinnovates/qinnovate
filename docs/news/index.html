Intelligence Briefs

NIST Post-Quantum Cryptography Standards Finalized

Quantum Computing 2026-02

NIST has finalized three post-quantum cryptographic algorithms (ML-KEM, ML-DSA, SLH-DSA). These standards will directly impact BCI encryption protocols at QIF Band 3.

QIF Relevance: QIF Band 3 (Protocol Security) specifications should reference these PQC standards for forward-secure neural data encryption.

Synchron Stentrode Receives FDA Breakthrough Device Designation

Neurotechnology 2026-01

Endovascular BCI reaches another clinical milestone. The minimally invasive approach changes the attack surface analysis — no craniotomy means different physical security vectors.

QIF Relevance: QIF I0 (Neural Gateway) threat model needs endovascular-specific vectors alongside traditional implanted devices.

UNESCO Publishes Recommendations on Neurotechnology Ethics

Neuroethics & Policy 2026-01

First intergovernmental framework for neurotechnology governance. Addresses cognitive liberty, mental privacy, and neural data as a special category.

QIF Relevance: QIF Band 7 (Cognitive Sovereignty) aligns with UNESCO cognitive liberty principles. Framework validation opportunity.

First Adversarial EEG Attack Demonstrated at IEEE S&P

BCI Security 2026-01

Researchers demonstrate that adversarial perturbations in EEG signals can fool BCI decoders, causing misclassification of motor intent.

QIF Relevance: Directly validates QIF threat model: adversarial signal injection at Band 5 (Signal Processing). Coherence metric could detect these perturbations.
